Decibels

Decibels look weird as they use logarithms. They become just an exercise in conversion once you understand that every 3 dB is doubling of power. Assuming you start with 1 W, 3 dB increase will be 2 W; 6 dB increase will be 4 W; and 9 dB increase will be 8 W. Conveniently 10 dB is same as tenfold increase.

A single dB is around 20% of a value.

G5B01

A two-times increase or decrease in power results in a change of how many dB?

  • A. Approximately 2 dB
  • B. Approximately 3 dB
  • C. Approximately 6 dB
  • D. Approximately 12 dB

Every 3 dB we double the power.
